Frequently Asked Questions

What traffic sources do you accept? +

We accept a wide range of traffic sources including push notifications, popunders, native ads, social traffic, and in-app traffic. Our platform is optimized for mobile traffic, but we also support desktop sources in select verticals. All traffic must comply with our quality guidelines and carrier policies.

How does the integration process work? +

Our integration process typically takes 3-5 business days. After an initial consultation, our team provides you with API access, creative assets, and technical documentation. We'll set up a test campaign to ensure everything is working properly before scaling to full production. Our technical team offers support throughout the entire process.

What payment models are available? +

We offer several monetization models including CPA (cost per acquisition), revenue share (up to 80%), and hybrid models. For high-volume partners, we can also provide minimum guarantees. Payment terms are weekly or bi-weekly depending on volume, with multiple payout methods available including wire transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.

Which countries do you monetize? +

We currently monetize traffic in 58 countries across North America, Europe, MENA, APAC, and LATAM. Our strongest performing geos include the US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Australia, and Brazil. We regularly expand to new markets and can provide a complete list of supported countries upon request.

How often do you process payouts? +

We process payouts on a weekly basis for established partners, with payments typically sent every Monday for the previous week's earnings. New partners start with bi-weekly payouts for the first month. There is a minimum payout threshold of $500, and earnings below this amount roll over to the next payout period. We offer various payment methods including wire transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.
